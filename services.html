What We Offer

Professional Services Built on Experience

From ground-up new construction to targeted repairs, Stonebridge brings expertise, integrity, and craftsmanship to every project we take on.

Design / Build

Our design/build process always starts by building a strong relationship with our clients. Using a general contractor such as Stonebridge for design/builds is a great way to streamline your project, reduce costs, and maintain a single point of accountability from concept through completion.

Rather than managing an architect, a designer, and a builder separately, our integrated approach means fewer miscommunications, faster timelines, and a smoother experience for you. We listen first, then design and build with your priorities in mind at every step.

Whether you're starting from a napkin sketch or an architect's blueprints, we work with you to develop a practical, beautiful plan and then execute it with precision and care.

Renovation & Remodeling

Renovation & Remodeling

At Stonebridge, we've seen a thing or two. Utilizing a mix of hard-earned construction wisdom, creativity, and determination, we help our clients transform their homes and spaces into something they're proud of.

Whether it's opening up a floor plan, updating an outdated kitchen, finishing a basement, or completely reimagining a space, we approach every renovation with the same standards we apply to new builds: quality materials, skilled tradespeople, and attention to every detail.

We work collaboratively with homeowners throughout the process, ensuring you're informed and comfortable at every stage—no surprises, no shortcuts.

General Contractor

Stonebridge Construction LLC is a master of craft. We have built homes of every shape and size. Whether you're building a sleek modern ranch or a rustic three-story cabin, our team has the experience, the network of trusted subcontractors, and the project management discipline to deliver.

As your general contractor, we manage every aspect of your project—scheduling, subcontractors, material procurement, inspections, and quality control—so you don't have to. You get one point of contact, clear communication, and a team that treats your project like their own.

Stonebridge hires only the highest-quality subcontractors, which means, from end to end, our clients get the best of the best.

Home Repairs

Home Repairs

Stonebridge handles repairs of all sizes. From small cracks in masonry and cosmetic fixes to structural corrections and storm damage remediation, no job is too small to receive our full attention.

We approach every repair with the same high standards that define our larger projects: proper diagnosis, quality materials, and workmanship that lasts. We don't patch problems—we solve them.

For homeowners who want to maintain the integrity and value of their investment, having a trusted contractor on call makes all the difference. Stonebridge is that contractor.
